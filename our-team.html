    Demitri and Karen have served together in ministry since 1993, first with Christian Surfers South Africa and later with Calvary Chapel. Establishing the church in the inner-city of Cape Town, they have seen Christ miraculously saving and transforming people from all walks of life, including prostitutes, criminals, drug addicts, refugees and the homeless.
